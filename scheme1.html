UP Scholarship Scheme 2025-26 : Short Details

Social Welfare Department, Uttar Pradesh has released the notification for UP Scholarship 2025-26 for Class 9–10 (Pre-Matric), Class 11–12 (Post-Matric), and Post-Matric Other Than Intermediate. Students can apply online within the given dates below.

Application Fee

✅ No Application Fee for any candidate.

Eligibility

  • Applicants must be Domicile of Uttar Pradesh.
  • Pre-Matric: Students enrolled in Class 9th or 10th.
  • Post-Matric: Students enrolled in Class 11th or 12th.
  • Dashmottar: Students enrolled in UG / PG / Diploma / Certificate Courses.

Documents Required

  • Last Exam Marksheet
  • Caste Certificate
  • Income Certificate
  • Bank Passbook
  • Fee Receipt
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport Size Photo

How to Apply

  • Visit the official UP Scholarship Portal (link below).
  • Register for the relevant category (Pre-Matric, Post-Matric, Dashmottar).
  • Login and fill in details carefully.
  • Upload required documents and submit before the deadline.
  • Take printout and submit hard copy to your institution.
