Haryana Deen Dayal Lado Lakshmi Yojana 2025 : Short Details

The Haryana Government has launched the Deen Dayal Lado Lakshmi Yojana to empower poor women by providing them ₹2,100 per month directly into their bank accounts. The scheme promotes women’s financial independence and aims to improve their living standards.

Important Dates

EventDate
Online Apply Start Date25 September 2025
Online Apply Last DateNot Yet Declared

Official Website: https://pension.socialjusticehry.gov.in/

Application Fee

No Application Fee is required for this scheme.

Eligibility Criteria

  • The applicant must be a permanent resident of Haryana.
  • She must be a woman aged 23 years or above.
  • She should not be employed in any government service.
  • Her family income must not exceed ₹1,40,000 per year.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Residence/Domicile Certificate
  • Family ID (PPP)
  • Bank Account Details
  • Income Certificate
  • Passport-size Photograph
  • Proof of Age (Birth/School Certificate)

How To Apply Online

  1. Visit the official website of the Haryana Government.
  2. Go to the “Haryana Deen Dayal Lado Lakshmi Yojana 2025–26” section.
  3. Read all instructions carefully before applying.
  4. Click on “Apply Online”.
  5. Register or log in using your mobile number and Aadhaar.
  6. Fill all required details correctly and upload documents.
  7. Review and submit the application.
  8. Download or print the acknowledgment slip.

Important Questions

Q: What is the Haryana Deen Dayal Lado Lakshmi Yojana 2025?
A: It’s a welfare scheme by the Haryana Government to provide ₹2,100/month financial aid to poor women.

Q: Who can apply?
A: Women permanent residents of Haryana aged 23 years or above, not in government service, and with annual income ≤ ₹1,40,000.

Q: How is the money transferred?
A: Through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) directly into the beneficiary’s bank account.
