How To Download Admit Card

  1. Visit the official PGCIL website.
  2. Go to the “Careers / Job Opportunities” section.
  3. Find the “Field Engineer and Field Supervisor Recruitment 2025” notice.
  4. Click on the Admit Card / Call Letter link.
  5. Login using your registration number / application ID and password / date of birth.
  6. Download and save the admit card PDF.
  7. Print a hard copy for the examination day.
