Eligibility Criteria

  • Senior Secondary Teacher (Class 11–12, Paper-II): Postgraduate degree in the subject with Second Division + B.Ed (not required for Computer Science). SC/ST/EBC/BC/Divyang get 5% relaxation.

How To Download Admit Card

  1. Visit the official Bihar STET website.
  2. Go to “Notifications / Admit Card / Latest Updates”.
  3. Click on the STET 2025 Exam Date / Admit Card link.
  4. Enter your Application Number / Registration ID and Date of Birth / Password.
  5. Submit and view the Admit Card.
  6. Download and save the PDF for future reference.
  7. Print at least one clear copy for the exam day.
