How To Download Admit Card

  1. Visit the official RBI website.
  2. Go to “Opportunities @ RBI” section, then “Current Vacancies” & “Call Letters”.
  3. Find the link for “RBI Grade B Phase-I Admit Card 2025”.
  4. Enter your Registration Number / Roll Number, Password / Date of Birth, and captcha.
  5. Click “Login” and view the admit card.
  6. Download, save, and print the admit card.
  7. Carry the printed admit card and a valid photo ID to the exam center.
