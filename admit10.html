UPSSSC Junior Assistant Admit Card 2025 – Download Now

The Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released the Admit Card for Junior Assistant 2025 Examination. Candidates who have registered can download their admit cards from the official website.

Important Dates

EventDate
Application Start12 September 2023
Last Date to Apply03 October 2023
Exam Date29 June 2025
Admit Card Available25 June 2025

How to Download Admit Card

  • Visit the official UPSSSC website (https://upsssc.gov.in).
  • Click on the “Download Admit Card” section.
  • Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth.
  • Click on “Submit”.
  • Your Admit Card will appear on the screen — download and print it.
